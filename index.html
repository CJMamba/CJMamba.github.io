Beam Struggle

Goku and Vegeta have always been rivals since the first battle they ever had. But now it comes to an end. Who will win in this epic clash Kamehameha vs Big Bang Attack?

This animation used multiple different sprite poses of Goku and Vegeta and classic tweens on multiple frames to make them move along with the usage of brightness and glow filters to create the beams fired.








Bart's Do a Barrel Roll

Bart Simpson has gone absolutely insane somehow 2 clones of himself to spin his head around for no apparent reason.

This animation used multiple frames, onion skinning, and scaffolding to align the elements of the head and face in place to ensure a smooth rotation.








Raindrop

A raindrop somehow defies gravity as it continues to drop down and somehow come right back only to drop back down and repeat the process for infinity.

This animation used onion skinning to create the thinning of the raindrop as the force of impact came closer to the ground and repeated in reverse for it come back up.








Maltruant's Demise

Maltruant has finally been imprisoned and sentenced to death. In order to catch him off guard, the Universal Force led by one Ben Tennyson secretly send him a exploding gift.

This animation used multiple frameworks and the use of symbols and classic tweenings. Using action script code, I was able to create the button at the title screen.
